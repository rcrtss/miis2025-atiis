  ---
  Limitations

  - L1 — Data layer: LifeSnaps is a convenience sample; population_df = cohort not population; only 2 age bands; mid-percentiles only; no anthropometrics/bed-times; synthetic generator's
  AR(1)+mixing correlation structure is a simplification → data/DESIGN.md "Limitations"
  - L2 — DS.1 measurement: LLM-judge unvalidated (no inter-annotator agreement, no judge-accuracy check); rubric item subjectivity; single judge model → EVALUATION.md DS.1 limitations
  - L3 — DS.2 measurement: single-run stochasticity (one generation per (query,persona)); Sonnet-authored test suites, author-reviewed but not expert-authored (vs. paper's 7 data
  scientists).
  - L4 — DS.2 dimension definitions: paper gives no formal dimension definitions or selection rationale, so CE/DH/GP/OC boundaries were operationalized here; residual risk in boundary cases
  (CE-vs-OC) and unvalidated dimension selection (the formalization itself is a contribution → lives in M4.2.3; only the residual risk is the limitation)
  - L5 — Fidelity to the paper: DELTA schema divergence from paper schema; Approach-vs-Docstring resolution decision; ds_refined treated identically to ds_oneshot by the evaluator →
  EVALUATION.md "Important notes", data/data_schemas/DELTA.md
