\subsection{Research Questions}

The main question that this work aims to address is: to what extent do LLM-based judges reproduce the structure of FH blameworthiness in a multi-agent committee? We address this through two sub-questions:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{RQ1.} How does the blameworthiness assigned by the Friedenberg--Halpern committee framework vary across its parameters (belief, vote, switch cost, stakes, pressure effectiveness, threshold, and group size)?
  \item \textbf{RQ2.} Do LLM-based judges placed in the same committee scenario assign blame that varies, in direction and in shape, as the framework's predictions specify?
\end{itemize}
